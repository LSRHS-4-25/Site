Welcome to Charleston, South Carolina's largest city. Welcome to the site of the Battle of Fort Sumter - the first battle of the American Civil War, as well as the battle of Sullivan's Island where, during the American Revolutionary War, the American Colonists first proved that they could stand up to the far mor powerful british army.
