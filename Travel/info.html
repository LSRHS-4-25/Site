If you are looking for ways to get to Charleston, Charleston Airport is one of your quickest options. To plan your flight or recieve a flight itinerary, pleas visit their page. Charleston is an excellent road trip destination if you live in New England or the Mid-Atlantic Washington area. Recently, my family took a road trip from Sudbury, MA, and we made stops in Philadelphia, Hershey, PA, Charlottesville, VA, Charleston, and Clearwater, FL. I would highly recommend a road trip such as this one.
