Rave Reviews

If you're looking for a place to stay in Charleston, I would highly recommend the Harbourview Inn. This historic hotel, with a beautiful view of Charleston harbour, is located right in the heart of Charleston's waterfront park, and is in walking distance to many of Charleston's restaurants and bars. The Harbourview Inn offers a complimentary continental breakfast included in your stay. Also included in your stay are complimentary wine and cheese hour, as well as champagne and other beverages served in the rooftop lounge. A final amenitiy that the Inn has come to be known for is its complimentary cookies and milk starting at 9:00pm. This hotel is kid friendly and has luxurious rooms starting at just 180$/night.

The Harbourview's rooms feature free wifi, tempurpedic matresses, and grand views of Charleston Harbour, The Waterfront Park, and historic Queen Street. The Inn's parkside rooms feature Juliett Balconies with views of Queen Street, accessed through French Doors. The Harbourside Suites feature a private balcony with views of Charleston Harbour's landmarks, as well as complimentary snacks delivered to the room throughout the day.
