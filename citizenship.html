CITIZENSHIP

cit·i·zen·ship

ˈsidizənˌSHip

noun

the character of an individual viewed as a member
of society; behavior in terms of the duties, obligations,
and functions of a citizen: an award for good citizenship.
LS WEBSITE

Citizenship is important both on an international level, locally in your own community, and even digitally online. I believe that honesty, compassion, respect, responsibility, and courage are necessary traits in a good citizen because in order to earn the kindness, and loyalty of other people, you must be kind and considerate to them also. On this site, you will learn the importance of citizenship in all aspects of life. To read the citizenship contract, see the link below.

More on Citizenship

Being a good citizen online is important because, if everyone feels safe online, the internet can be a place where people can work, play, and grow happily. Being willing to listen is another trait of a good citizen. willingness to listen is important because you must listen to, consider, and respect other points of view online as well as in real life to ensure that people will do the same to you.

Citizenship IRL:

To be a good citizen online, one must be a good citizen in real life also. Treating people with compassion is important, and respecting other people, even those you don't know, can help to make the world we live in better.
