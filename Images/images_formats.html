Images

There are three main types of images online JPEG, GIF, and PNG. Each of these types of images has its own use and purpose.

___________________________________________________________________

JPEG images work well with photography or other images that you get online. These are the most common.

GIF images are often short, repeating, movies or animations (about 0.5-5 seconds long).

PNG images are the least commonly used, however they are useful if you want to use a transparent background image.
